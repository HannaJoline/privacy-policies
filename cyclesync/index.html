CycleSync was built with one principle: your cycle data is yours alone. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics. Everything stays on your device.

1. Overview

CycleSync is a menstrual cycle and symptom tracking app designed to help you understand your body's patterns. The app uses locally stored data and on-device algorithms to predict cycle phases and provide insights.

This privacy policy explains what data the app handles, how it's stored, and your rights regarding that data.

2. What Data Does the App Store?

CycleSync stores the following information that you choose to enter:

  • Daily symptom logs: energy level, mood, sleep quality, libido, appetite, skin condition, body symptoms, discharge, and free-text notes
  • Cycle data: estimated cycle length, phase predictions, and historical patterns
  • App preferences: display settings and notification preferences

All of this data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). It never leaves your phone.

3. What Data Do We Collect?

None. Absolutely zero. We do not collect, transmit, receive, or have access to any of your data.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, age, etc.)
  • Health or cycle data
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

4. Third-Party Services

CycleSync does not use or integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media SDKs
  • No cloud storage or sync services

The app makes zero network requests. It works fully offline.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security:

  • Data is stored in your device's local database (SwiftData/Core Data)
  • Protected by your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID
  • Encrypted at rest by iOS device encryption
  • Sandboxed â€” other apps cannot access CycleSync's data

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app permanently removes all data
  • Individual entries can be deleted within the app at any time
  • There are no backups on our end â€” we have no servers
  • If you use iCloud device backups, your app data may be included in those backups (managed by Apple, not by us)

7. Children's Privacy

CycleSync is not directed at children under 13. The app does not collect any information from any user, regardless of age. However, the app's content (menstrual cycle tracking) is designed for adolescents and adults.

8. Your Rights

Because we don't collect any data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are handled entirely on your device. You have full control:

  • Access: All your data is visible to you in the app at all times
  • Deletion: Delete individual entries or uninstall the app to remove everything
  • Portability: Your data lives on your device and in your device backups
  • No opt-out needed: There's nothing to opt out of â€” we collect nothing

9. Frequently Asked Questions

Can you see my cycle data?
No. We have no servers, no database, and no way to access your data. Everything you enter stays on your phone and nowhere else.
Does the app send data over the internet?
No. CycleSync makes zero network requests. It works completely offline. You could put your phone in airplane mode forever and the app would work exactly the same.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
It's gone permanently. Since we don't store anything on servers, deleting the app means all your symptom logs and cycle data are removed from your device. There is no way to recover it.
Does the app use Apple HealthKit?
No. CycleSync does not integrate with HealthKit or any other Apple health frameworks. Your data stays entirely within the app.
Is my data included in iCloud backups?
If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your app data may be included as part of your full device backup. This is managed by Apple and protected by your Apple ID. We have no access to these backups.
Do you use any analytics or tracking?
No. We don't use Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, Facebook SDK, or any other analytics or tracking tool. We have zero insight into how you use the app.
Will you ever start collecting data?
Privacy-first is a core design principle of CycleSync, not just a feature. If this ever changes (it won't), we would update this policy and require your explicit consent before any data collection.
How does the prediction algorithm work without collecting data?
The cycle prediction runs entirely on your device using a Bayesian algorithm that learns from the data you've logged locally. No cloud computing, no AI servers â€” just math running on your phone.
Is this app GDPR compliant?
Yes. Since we don't collect, process, or store any personal data, CycleSync is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. There is simply no data to regulate.
Who can I contact with questions?
You can reach us at jolinebergsjo@gmail.com. We're happy to answer any privacy-related questions.
